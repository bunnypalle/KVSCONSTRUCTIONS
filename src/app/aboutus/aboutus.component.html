About Us

We KVS CONSTRUCTION COMPANY are proud to introduce ourselves, as one of the leading Industrial Construction Companies based in the GCC, and the well-known Indian based Construction Company.


We have established KVS CONSTRUCTION COMPANY and started Operations in 2019. Within a short span of time, we have successfully executed prestigious projects in Cement, Waste Gas Recovery System, Lime and Steel Industry for our esteemed Customers.


Our Electro-Mechanical Division's capability includes fabrication and erection of structural buildings of any size and demonstrated potential to handle erection and commissioning of all types of Industrial Plant and machinery for Cement, Oil & Gas, Off-Shore & On-Shore pipe lines, Steel, Minerals, Cement, Paper and Power Sectors.


KVS CONSTRUCTION COMPANY have their own fleet of Cranes and Earth moving equipment, contemporary tools and tackles required for mechanical equipment installation besides significant quantity of scaffolding materials.

1.KVS CONSTRUCTION COMPANY have a team of dedicated supervisors and managerial professionals ably supported by a well experienced and talented crew of supporting staff, draftsmen, technicians and skilled labour force to meet the challenges of Mega sized Industrial projects in the Middle East and ready to move at a short notice period, if required.

2.KVS CONSTRUCTION COMPANY's Civil construction division is well equipped and having experience and expertise to handle piling, shoring, dewatering, water proofing, slip-form concerting for industrial construction jobs besides the conventional sub and super structured concreting and all the related jobs.

3. ENGINEERING EXPERTS

  • Fabrication fo 12,000 MT of steel
  • Erection of Plant & Machinery of 25,000MT
  • Civil Concerting of 60,000M
  • Installation and commissioning of Electrical Sub Stations of both high and medium voltage, power distribution networks including cabling for complete plant automation.
